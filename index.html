Welcome to the Hub

This educational hub empowers individuals with digital skills for remote work opportunities, fostering economic growth and decent employment in the digital age.

Key Statistics

  • By 2030, workers can expect 39% of their existing skill sets to transform or become outdated.
  • Broadening digital access is the most transformative trend, with 60% of employers expecting significant impact.
  • In Latin America, remote work has surged from 3% pre-pandemic to 30% by 2025.
  • As of January 2025, 29.4% of paid workdays in the US are performed fully from home.
  • Digital literacy significantly improves individual income levels and narrows income gaps.
